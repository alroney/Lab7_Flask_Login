About Zoey

Zoey is a rescue.

How I Groom Zoey

  1. Deshedder: Removing any tangles and loose clumps of hair.
  2. Wire Brush: Brushing with light force to tackle the loose strands of hair.
  3. Flea Comb: This can come first to check fleas before grooming. Used as last step for removing small debris missed from the previous brushes.
